MINT CHOCOLATE CAKE RECIPE

Ingridients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tbs baking powder
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp peppermint extract
  • Full-fat buttermilk

instructions

  • Preheat oven to 350F,grease backing pans and dust with cocoa powder
  • Place all dry ingridients into the bowl,stir to combine
  • In onother bowl whisk all wet ingridients
  • Add wet ingridients to dry and mix
  • Pour evenly into prepared pans
  • put the pan in the oven for30-35minutes
  • When ready cool for ten minutes in the pan then turn ontoba wire rack to cool completely
