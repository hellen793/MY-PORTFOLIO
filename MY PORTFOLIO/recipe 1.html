VEGAN VODCA PASTA

Ingridients

  • Olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Quater cup of Vodca
  • Full fat coconut milk
  • Pasta
  • Salt,black pepper,
  • Water

Instuctions

  • Heat the olive oil over low heat,add the garlic and saute until fragrant
  • Add the tomato paste stir until it's deep red in coor
  • Add the vodca,then the coconut milkand salt,let the mxture simmer
  • Boil the pasta seperately for 10-15 minutes
  • Add the boiled pasta to your mixture
  • Mix well then serve while hot!
